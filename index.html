Scott Kingsley

Creating Beauty, elegance, and simplicity from complexity.

Web design, web development, UI/UX, they aren’t just about writing code. The biggest challenge those roles face is in problem solving. It’s about working with the stakeholder and understanding the scope of the project, design limitations, framework requirements. What they don’t want at all and what they feel they can only wish for. Once these items are discussed, winnowed down, expanded and settled can the coding begin. Coding poses its own challenges, but with the gameplan formulated, the coding challenges become solvable bumps along the way. I approach these challenges with enthusiasm, determination and relish the opportunity to meet the challenge.
